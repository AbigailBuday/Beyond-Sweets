Delicious, Comforting, Creamy Roasted Mushroom Soup.

A bowl of warmth that drives away your chills. Roasted for warmth Farm-fresh Shiitake and Button mushrooms are delicately roasted to bring out their full flavor, then simmered for hours in real cream, butter, chicken stock and our chef’s blend of herbs & spices until it’s thick and savory. includes one oven-fresh baguettini.
